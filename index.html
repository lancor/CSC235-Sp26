Key Figures of the Web

The following individuals played a major role in the development of the World Wide Web and HTML.

  1. Tim Berners-Lee – Inventor of the World Wide Web
  2. Vannevar Bush – Concept of hypertext (Memex)
  3. Marc Andreessen – Mosaic browser
  4. Robert Cailliau – Early WWW development

Key Figures (Images)

Tim Berners-Lee

Invented the World Wide Web and wrote the first web browser/editor.

Vannevar Bush

Proposed the Memex concept, an early inspiration for hypertext.

Marc Andreessen

Co-created Mosaic, an early popular graphical web browser.

Robert Cailliau

Collaborated on early WWW work and helped promote the project.
